I am a Ph.D. student at Texas A&M University in Industrial and systems engineering department working with Dr. Yu Ding in the Advanced Metrology Lab. My research focuses on building data-driven models for engineering systems, particularly wind farms and turbines. I have also worked briefly on statistical modeling of extreme events with applications in hydrology.

I am interested in statistics and optimization in general.
